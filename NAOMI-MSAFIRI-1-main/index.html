RECTOR WELCOME NOTE

Welcome Note from the Rector

Dear Graduates, Faculty, Families, and Friends, It is my great honor and pleasure to welcome you to the 2024 Convocation Ceremony of Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre. Today, we celebrate the outstanding achievements of our graduates who have worked tirelessly to reach this milestone. Your dedication, resilience, and hard work have brought you to this pivotal moment, and we are immensely proud of each and every one of you. As you embark on new journeys, I encourage you to continue striving for excellence, embracing challenges, and making a positive impact in the world. Congratulations to the Class of 2024! Today, we celebrate your success and look forward to the incredible contributions you will make in the future. With warmest regards,

Dr. Tumaini Katunzi

Rector, Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre
